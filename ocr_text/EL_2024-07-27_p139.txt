       Also possible is that Lewis was introduced to the Loguen family through

mutual friends. Frederick Douglass was one of Lewis’ earliest supporters, and there is

evidence that she was connected to the entire family. Reverend Loguen hosted

Frederick Douglass Jr. and his wife Virginia Hewlett Douglass in the week prior to

Lewis’ visit. The couple were newly-weds, married on August 4, 1869, in Cambridge,

Massachusetts.267 Their time in Massachusetts and Lewis’ visit to Massachusetts may

have overlapped. Certainly, William Lloyd Garrison, and others who knew the

Douglass family, might have been in attendance. Also noteworthy is that Frederick

Jr.’s older brother Lewis Henry married Reverend Loguen’s daughter, Helen Amelia,

that same year.268 In December 1879, Charles Remond Douglass delivered the

presentation address at Shiloh Presbyterian Church for the unveiling of Edmonia
