collective action and commemorative practice, one that highlights the diverse

perspectives that animated the post-bellum, Pre-Harlem period. By centering Edmonia

Lewis’ interactions with African American leaders and writers, I also hope to offer a

more layered reading of nineteenth-century Black culture, one that is attentive to Paul
