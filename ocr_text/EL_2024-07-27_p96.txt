correspondence. Was Child’s criticism of Lewis’ money management, which she

blamed on being raised by “poor negros” and “wild Indians” an honest assessment, or

did her racism blind her the financial difficulties Lewis faces early in her career?180

representation of Lewis’s character as a young businesswoman?181 Or would Lewis’
