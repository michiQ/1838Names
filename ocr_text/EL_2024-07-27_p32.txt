       In this chapter, I will do a close reading of the pamphlet, How Edmonia Lewis

Became an Artist.30 I argue that How Edmonia Lewis Became an Artist represents

Edmonia Lewis’ effort to create a persona that would appeal to the public imagination.

The last line in How Edmonia Lewis Became an Artist reads as follows: “God's gift to

Edmonia Lewis is unconquerable energy, as well as genius; and these two combined

enable her to rise above all prejudices of race or color, and command the respect and

honor of all true lovers of art.”31 This concluding sentence points to the possibility that

Lewis took inspiration from articles that were written about her and that this was a co-

authored/co-edited work. In 1869, just around the time that How Edmonia Lewis

Became an Artist might have been written, Lewis returned to America for the first

time since she left in 1865.32 One of her first recorded stops was at the office of the
