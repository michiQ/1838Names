and other experiences when he met Lewis. And if Douglass was privy to the poisoning

incident, and that Lewis was badly assaulted by vigilantes after the fact, then there is

almost no doubt that they would have discussed what it meant to be Black in America,

and the benefits of leaving. For all that Lewis said in the media that might not be true

to her experience, her connections to her Black contemporaries point to the possibility

that she was aware of darker and more inconvenient truths.
