Free Church Baptists, was the first integrated church in America. With its many rooms

gathered to do important work.309 As the first African and Native American woman

sculptor, Edmonia Lewis’ presentation to Rev. Grimes added to the extensive list of

noteworthy events that took place at Tremont Temple. By presenting “Forever Free”

to Leonard Grimes, at his church, Lewis brought art, Black women’s activism,

performance, religious practice, and culture together, creating a new unique aspect to

the dynamic role of the Black church in culture formation.
