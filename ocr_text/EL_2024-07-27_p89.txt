traits in Lewis will place new emphasis on personal agency. What if rather than a

“girl” who was helped along by influential white abolitionists, Edmonia Lewis was

self-determining young woman with an excellent education, who sought out the

support and mentorship she needed to have a robust career? How would future

scholarship on Lewis change if we viewed her education as that of a scholar-activist?
