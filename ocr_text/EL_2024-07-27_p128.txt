between 1847 and 1852, at which point he headed west to participate in the Gold

Rush. Lewis was not yet ten years old.246 She was in school between 1858 and 1864

while her brother traveled across the United States and Europe.247 In 1868, about three

years after Lewis settled in Europe, Samuel settled in Bozeman, a frontier town in

what was then the new Territory of Montana. Evidence exists that the siblings were in

contact at some point during 1870, around the time Edmonia came to the United States

to exhibit her statue Hagar in Chicago. It is almost certain that Samuel would have

shared details of his travels with his younger sister and provided advice when she

made her own travel plans. We know through written testimony that Frederick

Douglass and other well-traveled allies had encouraged Edmonia to move to Italy, so

it is hard to believe Samuel did not play a role supporting his sister in this major

transition.
