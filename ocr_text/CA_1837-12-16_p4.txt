DUTIES OF CHRISTIANS WHO ARF IN DEBT.

1. Let them ascertain the full amount of
liabilities, and whether they have failed to
fulfil their engagements, or only fear that

matter before their creditors. Let them im-

No matter whether the debtor be actually in-
solvent, or not; no matter whether derange-

3. Let Christians purchase nothing but
what is absolutely indispensable. and regard
their promise to pay, just as sacredly as they
would their oaths: let Christians remember
that they are justas much bound to pay their
notes, on the day they promise, as they are
to speak the truth in a court of justice.

fiom the unchristian system of speculations.
