permitted, and even seen as beneficial for the development of students.197

       In her first interview with Lydia Maria Child in 1864, just about a years after

who was training her: “The man who made a bust of John Brown must be a friend to

my people.”198 That same year, Lewis would sell medallions of John Brown to fund

her transition to Rome for additional education. Later in her career, she completed a
