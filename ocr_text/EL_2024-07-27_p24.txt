     Black Archives, Black Print: A Methodology for Studying an Artist Life

       There are two substantial archives we can use to explore the life of Edmonia

Lewis. The first is her sculptures. Art historians have used her sculptures to explore

the racist beliefs underpinning the neoclassical style, and the limitations this style

placed on Lewis’ work. The subject matter of her sculptures have been used to make

arguments on the extent to which Lewis’ identity played a role in her artistic

expression.21 The second is the archive of print media written about Lewis during her

career, which has been referenced for biographical information, but has not yet been

appreciated from the perspective of literary scholarship.22 Edmonia Lewis was of

particular interest to what I will call the pro-Black press, because her accomplishments

served as support for the political arguments they sought to advance. Through the pro-
