       In the same issue of the Pacific Appeal that posted Edmonia’s advertisement,

there was an announcement that “a committee of ladies and gentlemen” gave a

reception for Lewis.283 They held this reception at the lecture room of Third Baptist

Church on Powell Street on the Monday following the opening exhibition. Third

Baptist Church, now a historic landmark, has been a cornerstone of the Black

community since San Francisco was in its nascent years. Formerly named the First

Colored Church of San Francisco, Third Baptist was founded in 1852 and was

originally located on Dupont Street between Greenwich and Filbert.284 Third Baptist

was amongst three Black churches that served the religious, social, political, and even

economic needs of the community. As a physical space, the church served as a multi-

purpose meeting space, offering educational and recreational programming.285 It

follows that Third Baptist would see the value in celebrating the success of Lewis, as
