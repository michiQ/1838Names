cegradation upon the people of color. An accurate de-
velopement of these has long been felt to be a desideratum.
Public attention has tong been directed to the inequality of
privileges between the white and colored man in regard
to the privilege of voting, as thongh that were the only
disability; but that is only onc among many, some of which

apart. Our principle is, to take off all artificial clogs
